Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a certificate of completion?

Once you complete 100% of a course, your certificate of completion will be available for download from the course dashboard. You can find it under the "Certificate" tab.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on most course purchases. If you are unsatisfied with a course for any reason, you can request a full refund within 30 days of your purchase date through your account settings.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, you can click the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. We will send a password reset link to the email address associated with your account.
